# Location

## Schloss Dagstuhl - Leibniz Center for Informatics

The seminar will be held at [Schloss Dagstuhl](https://www.dagstuhl.de), the world-renowned research center for computer science located in Wadern, Germany.

### About Schloss Dagstuhl

Schloss Dagstuhl is a castle in the Saarland region of Germany that has been transformed into a unique center for computer science research. It provides an ideal environment for scientific seminars, featuring:

- Modern conference facilities
- Comfortable accommodation on-site
- Peaceful setting conducive to focused discussion
- Library and research resources
- Excellent dining and social spaces

## Travel Information

### Getting to Dagstuhl

#### By Air

The nearest major airports are:

- **Frankfurt Airport (FRA)** - Approximately 200 km away
- **Luxembourg Airport (LUX)** - Approximately 90 km away
- **Saarbrücken Airport (SCN)** - Approximately 50 km away

#### By Train

The nearest train station is **Wadern**, which is about 5 km from Schloss Dagstuhl. From major cities:

- From Frankfurt: Train to Saarbrücken, then to Wadern
- From Luxembourg: Bus or train connections available

#### From the Train Station

Taxi or arranged shuttle service is available from Wadern station to Schloss Dagstuhl.

### Detailed Travel Instructions

Comprehensive travel instructions are available on the [Dagstuhl website](https://www.dagstuhl.de/travel).

## Accommodation

All participants will be accommodated on-site at Schloss Dagstuhl. The center provides:

- Single rooms with private bathrooms
- WiFi access
- All meals included
- Common areas for informal discussions

## Visa Information

International participants may require a visa to enter Germany. Please check visa requirements for your country and apply well in advance. Invitation letters can be requested from the organizers.

## Local Information

### Climate

Early April weather in Saarland is typically mild with temperatures ranging from 5-15°C (41-59°F). We recommend bringing layers and rain gear.

### What to Bring

- Business casual attire for sessions
- Comfortable clothes for informal discussions
- Laptop for work sessions
- Any materials relevant to your research
