# Location

## Schloss Dagstuhl - Leibniz Center for Informatics

The seminar will be held at [Schloss Dagstuhl](https://www.dagstuhl.de), a research institute for computer science located in Wadern, Germany.

Schloss Dagstuhl is a castle in the Saarland region of Germany that has been transformed into a unique center for computer science research. 

### Getting to Dagstuhl
The Dagstuhl website provides detailed instructions on how to reach the venue: [Getting to Dagstuhl](https://www.dagstuhl.de/en/guests/how-to-get-to-schloss-dagstuhl).
